Utah County Milkshake Review by Melia Robison

I grew up in a small Idaho town and our milkshake options were pretty limited to Chick-Fil-A, Wendy's, and Mcdonald's. I always loved these shakes, at least until I came to Utah County. With various diners and burger shack's the shake options are endless here. Chick-Fil-A is still pretty high on my list (how could it not be) ESPECIALLY their Autumn Spice Shake. I'm not the only one who agrees either...

However, Mcdonald's and Wendy's have definitely been bumped down on my list of favorites... by A LOT. Here are a few key players in the Utah County shake game and my rankings for them!
